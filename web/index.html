        Welcome to Thai Trainer

        Your personal Thai tutor: 819 vocabulary words, 60+ consonants & vowels, and SM‑2 spaced repetition — all in one app.

        This quick tour highlights the key features. Find it again any time under Other → How to use.

        📚 Flashcards & SRS Review

        Start with Flashcards Thai→English (key 1). Reveal the answer, then rate yourself 1–5.

        The SM‑2 algorithm schedules each card's next review so you see it right before you'd forget it. SRS Review (key 4) shows only what's due today.

        ✨ Tap Thai text to explore

        In any flashcard or vocab list, tap the Thai word to open its detail card — romanisation, audio, and an example sentence in context.

        Then tap the Thai word inside the card to decompose it into syllable clusters. Every character is labelled and hoverable.

        สวัสดี
        สวัสดี — "hello" · tap to decompose each cluster

        📖 Vocab List & Script

        Vocab List (key V) shows all 819 words — search, filter by category, tap any word for the detail card with decomposition.

        Script cards (keys 5–9) teach consonants and vowels with SRS. Tone Drill (T) plays audio and asks you to identify the tone class.
